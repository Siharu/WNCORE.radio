Maintaining
the Signal.

Founded in 2012 by a small team of systems engineers and radio enthusiasts, WNCORE began as a personal project to aggregate shortwave frequencies across Southeast Asia. Twelve years later, we index over 12,000 verified stations across 310 countries.

We do not run proprietary tracking software. The frequencies provided are pulled directly from the public domain. Our core philosophy is simple: the signal belongs to everyone.

All station data is sourced from the Radio Browser community database. WNCORE contributes verified station metadata back to the commons on a weekly basis.
