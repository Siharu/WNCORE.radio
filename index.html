Another Sky — Original Fiction

Another Sky is an original post-apocalyptic sci-fi story set in 2032. Follow Som, a Bangladeshi civil engineer who discovers he has been transferred to an alternate Earth where a zombie outbreak has devastated 64% of humanity, the sky rains black Obsedia, and nine people closest to him have been erased from existence.

WNCORE Survivor Network

An interactive real-time chat system where survivors of the Another Sky world exchange intelligence. Features AI characters including Lars, Zahara, Mateo, Ji-Woo, Finn, Dmitri, Suhana, Ingrid, Yuki, Amara, and more.

Original Music

Original music tracks: Another Sky WWIII Lament, Obsedia no Kage, and Echoes of a Family.
