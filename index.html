About WNCORE

WNCORE is a global radio streaming network operating on 88.7 FM and its affiliated digital frequencies since 2016. We aggregate live audio streams from over 12,000 independent broadcasters across 195 countries — no algorithms, no recommendations, just radio.

We believe in the radio as a medium. Unlike curated playlists, radio is alive. It has context, locality, accident. A DJ in Lagos doesn't care about your listening history. A weather broadcast from rural Hokkaido carries something no streaming service can manufacture. We want to preserve that.

WNCORE does not serve ads. It does not sell data. The network is maintained by a small team of engineers and radio enthusiasts operating from an undisclosed location. We have been told, on multiple occasions, to shut down 88.7 FM. We have not complied.
