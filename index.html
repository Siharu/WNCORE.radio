About Our Music Sources

All music on the WNCORE Live Music channel is sourced from stations and archives that broadcast under open licenses including Creative Commons, public domain dedications, and royalty-free institutional streams. We do not host any audio files — all streams are pulled directly from their origin servers in real time.

Maintaining
the Signal.

Founded in 2016 by a small team of systems engineers and radio enthusiasts, WNCORE began as a personal project to aggregate and archive shortwave frequencies across the international broadcast band. Ten years later, we index over 12,000 verified stations across 310 countries.

We do not run proprietary tracking software. The frequencies provided are pulled directly from the public domain. Our core philosophy is simple: the signal belongs to everyone.

All station data is sourced from the Radio Browser community database. WNCORE contributes verified station metadata back to the commons on a weekly basis. We have no affiliations with any government, broadcaster, or regulatory body.

Note: Some signals in our archive cannot be verified. Node 09 has not responded since 2016. We do not know what is on 88.7.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025 · Node 09 Status: SILENT

Data Collection

WNCORE does not store personal data on our servers. All station metadata is sourced from public domain radio databases. Your listening history is stored locally in your browser only—we have no access to this information.

Exception: Unverified transmissions on 88.7 MHz may bypass standard routing and encryption.

Cookies & Tracking

We do not use tracking cookies, ad networks, or behavioral analytics. Your client-side preferences (dark mode, favorites) are stored in localStorage only.

Third-Party Services

Radio streams are pulled directly from their origin servers. We do not proxy, buffer, or intercept stream data. Some stations may include their own tracking—review their privacy policies independently.

Note: Data packets destined for Node 09 are handled by a separate, undocumented relay. Status unknown since 2016.

Questions?

Contact: privacy@wncoreradio.net
